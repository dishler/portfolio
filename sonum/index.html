Project Overview

Sonum is a music streaming service that puts the social aspect in the spotlight. Whereas Twitter, Facebook & Co are about sharing more than ever - music streaming is heading in the opposite direction: a simple exchange between person & algorithm.

The Challenge

The challenge of redesigning Sonum, a music streaming service, was to create a platform that emphasizes the social aspect of music sharing. While social media platforms like Twitter and Facebook focus on sharing various types of content, music streaming services often lack the social interaction between users and instead rely heavily on algorithms.

The Solution

Our solution involved stakeholder interviews to gather insights, followed by information architecture, wireframing, and visual design. We developed a design system for consistency and delivered a user-friendly, visually appealing product that met stakeholder goals.

My role in the project

As a key member of the Sonum project team, my primary responsibility revolved around UX research and design. Throughout the project, I closely collaborated with the business analyst and development team at OneClick Code (OCC) to establish intuitive user flows for the team invitation and acceptance feature. I took charge of creating wireframes and designing the UI for the native and web app, as well as the mobile web app. Additionally, I played a vital role in conducting various research activities, which included:
  • Stakeholder interview
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframes
  • Visual Design
  • Design system

Results and takeaways

The Sonum project yielded remarkable results, culminating in the successful launch of the platform's MVP version. Our team's diligent efforts brought the client's vision to reality, resulting in a functional and user-friendly music streaming platform. Sonum now offers robust features and capabilities that meet the needs of its users, providing a seamless and enjoyable music experience.
A key takeaway from this project is the significance of collaboration and open communication with the client. By actively involving the client throughout the design process and incorporating their feedback, we were able to deliver a product that exceeded their expectations. Additionally, our user-centered design approach proved critical in ensuring the platform's success, as we prioritized the needs and preferences of the users.
In conclusion, the Sonum project stands as a testament to the power of teamwork and user-centered design. The platform is now live and making a positive impact on music enthusiasts, fostering a vibrant and engaging music community. We take great pride in our contribution to this project and look forward to witnessing Sonum's continued growth and success in the future.
