    WORK PROCESS

    Briefing
    Research
    Prototyping
    Concept
    Transfer

    • Briefing

      At this stage I think over the structure of the site and the functionality of each page separately, based on the findings of the previous research

    • Research

      I conduct a selection of references, compose a mood board based on your preferences and the results of visual analysis of competitors, create a design concept for all pages and blocks in Figma

    • Prototyping

      We discuss the goals and objectives of your project, and ways to achieve them. The result of the briefing is the specification which describes the timing, cost, scope of work, and criteria

    • Concept

      I analyze the competitive environment and market of your product, conduct a visual analysis of the direction of your business, and find dependencies and patterns used in the design.

    • File Transfer

      I prepare a Figma file with a fully developed project, including adaptive versions for transferring to your developer
