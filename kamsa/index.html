Project Overview

Kamsa is a compensation management platform that provides real-time global salary data and expert support for compensation review and benchmarking. The platform aims to revolutionize the way companies compensate their most valuable asset - their people.

The Challenge

The HR/People and Finance leaders responsible for compensation management need a user-friendly platform that can simplify the complex process of compensation management and make it easy to use. The existing UI design of the Kamsa platform was not meeting the needs of these users, leading to a suboptimal user experience and inefficiencies in the compensation management process.

The Solution

The solution to this problem was to revamp the User Experience (UX) design of the Kamsa platform. The goal was to make the platform visually appealing and user-friendly. This was achieved by creating wireframes based on UX research results, which served as the foundation for a new UI design for the entire platform. The project concluded with the development of a sophisticated design system for a seamless user experience. The result was that Kamsa closed the gaps in compensation, helping companies pay their employees competitively and leading to a happier, more productive workforce.

My role in the project

My role in the project was focused on the design aspect of the Kamsa platform. I was responsible for creating wireframes, designing the UI, developing interactive prototypes, and creating the styleguide. I worked closely with the UX research team to ensure that the design was in line with the results of their research and met the needs of the end users. My goal was to deliver a visually appealing, intuitive, and user-friendly platform that would help HR/People and Finance leaders manage compensation effectively.

Results and takeaways

The project was a success with positive results. HR/People and Finance leaders were pleased with the improved visual design, making navigation and understanding easier. Thorough research and testing before launch helped identify and address potential issues, leading to success. The design system ensured consistency and professionalism. The project showcased the importance of UX design in creating a product that meets user needs and expectations, resulting in a more productive and satisfied workforce.
