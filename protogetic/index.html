Project Overview

The Protogetic is a portal with a Physical Security Products catalog, created for vendors and buyers. The goal of system to be: a central hub for all stakeholders (owners, architects, engineers, contractors, consultants, and vendors) who participate in the security building and infrastructure construction industries

The Challenge

The challenge in the design stage of Protogetic was to create a user-friendly platform for vendors and admins with clear data visualization and an intuitive Protogetic Filter. This filter had to reduce the time spent searching for important information, such as safety ratings, certifications, and detailed specifications, from hours to just a few seconds. Additionally, the platform had to cater to the diverse needs of all stakeholders in the security building and infrastructure construction industries by providing a central hub that is easy to navigate and access for all users, regardless of their technical proficiency or industry experience.

The Solution

The design stage of Protogetic aimed to create a user-friendly platform for vendors and admins in the security construction industry. A user-centered approach was taken, with a focus on interaction design, information architecture, and a flexible design system. Interactive prototypes were created for testing and improvement, and simple dashboards and admin systems were developed for ease of use. The result was a central hub with clear data visualization and an intuitive filter system.

My role in the project

I was responsible for designing the Amity platform from the ground up. I led the specification discovery process, analyzed user flows, and created the information architecture to ensure the platform met the needs of all stakeholders. I designed wireframes and the visual user interface (UI), focusing on usability and user experience to create an intuitive and easy-to-use solution. Throughout the project, I worked closely with the development team to ensure the platform was built according to the design specifications and met the requirements of the client. My attention to detail and dedication to creating a high-quality product resulted in a platform that effectively balances safety, convenience, and independence for all users.

Visual Design

RESPONSIVE VERSION

Our team implemented a responsive design solution for the Protogetic platform, ensuring optimal viewing experiences on all devices, from desktop computers to smartphones. The responsive design enhances the user experience and makes the platform more accessible, helping to attract and retain more users. This solution provides greater flexibility and allows users to access the platform anytime, anywhere, further supporting their needs and requirements.

Results and takeaways

Results:

  • A comprehensive and user-friendly platform was created for vendors and admins in the security construction industry
  • The platform meets the diverse needs of all stakeholders and provides a central hub for easy navigation and access
  • The platform features clear data visualization and an intuitive filter system that reduces time spent searching for information
  • The platform includes simple and straightforward dashboards and admin systems for managing and monitoring products and services
  • The platform is scalable and flexible, with a consistent visual language and user interface components that can be adapted and updated as needed

Takeaways:

  • User-centered design is essential for creating a platform that meets the needs and expectations of all stakeholders
  • Incorporating interactive prototypes in the design process allows for quick and iterative testing and improvement
  • Simple and intuitive dashboards and admin systems enhance the user experience and ease of use
  • A scalable and flexible design system accommodates future growth and changes in the industry.
